Issue 01 · April 2026 · Sample Edition
Cover Story

Why this issue exists, and the small stories we want to keep telling.

The Bloom Letter began as a one-off — a cover for a campus event flyer that turned into a six-page spread by accident. We kept going because the small stories on this campus deserve a home with margins and a typeface that takes them seriously.

This first issue is a sample of where we'd like to take it: a section for student-run businesses, a quiet column from the editor, and a quote from someone who'd usually never end up in print.

By Criste Joy Calosor · Editor

The library is the obvious answer. These five aren't, and one of them has the best pandesal within walking distance.
